Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the REA App accesses and uses your Google account data.

Data Access and Usage

The REA App accesses your Google Drive files only to perform the following three specific functions:

  1. Provide you with a list of users who have access to the files that you own or can access.
  2. Allow you to search and see which files a specific user has access to within your files.
  3. Allow you to remove access permissions for specific users from your files if needed.

Beyond these three functions, the app performs no additional operations on your data.

Data Storage and Sharing

The REA App does not store your data or any metadata about your files or users. It does not send any data to the app developer or any third party. All processing is done in real time through your session and stays within your Google account environment.

Security

The REA App only uses the permissions required to perform the described functions above and does not request or use unnecessary permissions.
